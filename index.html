Taylor Swift

Here's a time line of Taylor Swift's life:

  • 1989 - Born in Pennsylvania, United States
  • 2003 - Taylor traveled to Tennessee to pursue her dreams of becoming a singer.
  • 2006 - Taylor Swift's eponymous debut album was released.
  • 2008 - Taylor Swift received a "Best New Artist" nomination.
  • 2008 - Taylor released her second album, "Fearless."
  • 2009 - Taylor Swift's album, "Fearless," became the best selling album of 2009.
  • 2010 - Taylor Swift released her third album, "Speak Now."
  • 2012 - Taylor released the lead single of her fourth album, "Red" which becomes the first in Swift's career to peak at No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100.
  • 2012 - Taylor released her fourth album,"Red."
  • 2014 - Taylor's fifth album, 1989 debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard 200.
  • 2016 - Taylor wins her 21st career Billboard Music Award, for top touring artist, the most of any artist.
  • 2017 - Taylor releases her sixth album, "Reputation"
  • 2019 - Taylor releases her seventh album, "Lover"
  • 2020 - Taylor releases her eighth album, "Folklore"
  • 2021 - Taylor releases her ninth album, "Evermore"
  • 2021 - Taylor becomes the first female artist to win the "Brit Global Icon" award
